DIVE IN 

We are DREAMLAND THEATER established 2005 in Arlington, Virginia. We are unlike other theaters because our theme revolves around dreaming, all our movies are about or include dreams. We believe that dreams are a complex human mechanism that don't get as much attention as they should, and that is why we are raising awareness towards them. Since our launch date we have grown immensely and now have over 30 locations all throughout the United States. Our main focus is YOU! Owing to that fact, we have installed state of the art recliners; included with warming device and top notch sound quality, IMAX screens (72x90 ft), and a well equipped staff to assure you the best time possible. We want you and your loved ones to spend quality time with DREAMLAND THEATER If you are like us, and very curious about dreams this user-friendly website is just for you! You can find what movies are airing currently, the inside scoop with dream movie megastars, and even how and why dreams work. Click the links above to dive right in! 

Preface

Hey this is Dani here, I wanted to go a little more into explaining my multigenre research project, for this project, I will research the effect of dreams on society and how they are portrayed in pop culture, I will represent this using various different artifacts.For my repetent I chose to make a website for a movie theater that only broadcasts movies about dreams or including dreams. My website does only include dreams though, it has a mixture of a lot of things making it sort of a blog type of website mixed with a regular movie theater website. I put a lot of thought into my artifacts and wanted them to sort of flow from one another, while fitting the theme of a movie theater website. With this project I researched how dreams are represented in pop culture and learned how they played an important role in our society.
